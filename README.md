### About Me

I am a passionate creator and technologist who combines agriculture knowledge with modern technology. 

With a background in Agriculture and Marketing, I create practical AI tools, develop WordPress solutions, and share knowledge through YouTube and blogging. My mission is to empower farmers, creators, and rural youth with actionable skills in technology, digital growth, and online success.

As a WordCamp Speaker and Josh Talks speaker, I enjoy sharing my journey of building impactful work in both farming and tech spaces.
